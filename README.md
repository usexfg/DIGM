Three distinct tokens power the DIGM ecosystem, each with a specific and crucial role.

### **DIâ‚²M Coin: The Right to Host**

**DIâ‚²M** : (XFG-colored coin) key that unlocks the artist's ability to offer albums for sale in the DIGM audio marketplace. To sell music on the platform, it must be an album, publishing artist must hold sole copyrights, and must hold a DIâ‚²M coin. This mechanism serves several purposes:
