# DIGM Branding Guide

## Brand Identity

### DIGM ◉rigins (Mobile App)

**Name**: DIGM ◉rigins  
**Tagline**: "Authentic audio, cryptographically proven"  
**Purpose**: Record device-only audio with cryptographic proof of origin

**Visual Identity**:
- **Icon**: Circular waveform with ◉ symbol
- **Color Palette**: Monochrome (Ivory, Silver, Charcoal with marble texture)
- **Style**: Minimalist, premium, sophisticated
- **Platform**: iOS (with Android coming soon)

**Key Messages**:
- "Record with proof"
- "Device-authenticated audio"
- "Hardware-secured recordings"
- "Cryptographic provenance"

**Use Case**: Artists and creators recording audio consisting on device with verifiable proof.

---

### DIGM Platform (Web/Desktop)

**Name**: DIGM Platform  
**Tagline**: "The device-recorded audio marketplace"  
**Purpose**: Browse, purchase, and stream verified device-recorded albums

**Visual Identity**:
- **Icon**: DIGM logo
- **Primary Color**: Purple (main brand)
- **Secondary Color**: Green (verified content)
- **Accent Color**: Gold (premium features)
- **Platform**: Web, Desktop (Electron)

**Key Messages**:
- "Verifiable audio marketplace"
- "Device-recorded content only"
- "Cryptographic proof built-in"
- "Own your audio"

**Use Case**: Music discovery, purchase, and streaming platform for verified device-recorded audio.

## Application Ecosystem

### Relationships

```
DIGM Origins (Mobile)
    ↓ Records with proof
    ↓ Uploads to
    ↓
DIGM Platform (Web)
    ↓ Verifies proof
    ↓ Stores on
    ↓
Fuego Blockchain + Elderfier Network
```

### Workflow

1. **Record** in DIGM Origins (device microphone)
2. **Prove** with hardware signature (Secure Enclave)
3. **Upload** to DIGM Platform (web/desktop)
4. **Verify** proof signatures (cryptographic check)
5. **Publish** on blockchain (0x0B transaction)
6. **Stream** to listeners (licensed content)
