# DIGM Curator Revenue Sharing Model

## Overview

The DIGM platform introduces a revolutionary curator revenue model that incentivizes meaningful music discovery and high-quality playlist curation.

## Revenue Sharing Tiers

### 1. Standard Playlist
- **Artist Revenue Cut**: 10%
- **Listener Revenue Cut**: 10%
- **Total Curator Share**: 20%

### 2. High Engagement Playlist
- **Artist Revenue Cut**: 15%
- **Listener Revenue Cut**: 15%
- **Total Curator Share**: 30%
- **Qualification**: Demonstrates exceptional listener engagement

### 3. DIGM Discovery List
- **Artist Revenue Cut**: 25%
- **Listener Revenue Cut**: 25%
- **Total Curator Share**: 50%
- **Special Designation**: Playlist generates more streams for artist than all other sources

## Qualification Criteria

### High Engagement Playlist
- Listener engagement score > 75%
- High average listen duration
- Significant unique listener count

### DIGM Discovery List
- Playlist streams exceed artist's total streams from other sources by 50%
- Demonstrates significant artist exposure and discovery

## Economic Incentives

### Revenue Calculation
- Based on total streaming revenue
- Dynamically calculated per playlist
- Transparent, performance-driven model

### Additional Benefits
- CURA token minting for top-performing curators
- Governance weight based on curation performance
- Public recognition and playlist visibility

## Anti-Gaming Mechanisms
- Machine learning-based fraud detection
- Continuous performance monitoring
- Algorithmic adjustment of revenue sharing

- Total Playlist Revenue: $100
