# PARA Token Economics

## Overview

PARA is the utility and reward token of the DIGM ecosystem, designed to incentivize and reward platform participation across multiple roles.

## Total Supply
- **Total Supply**: 1 Quintillion (1,000,000,000,000,000,000) PARA tokens
- **Initial Distribution**: 99.99% over 30 years (999.9 Quadrillion)
- **Reserved for Future Emissions**: 0.01% (100 Billion)
- **Emission Pattern**: Front-loaded then evenly distributed
- **Design Goal**: Sustainable distribution over generations

## Annual Emission Schedule

### Emission Mechanism
- Initial Annual Emission: 1% of total supply
- Halving Period: Every 4 years
- Controlled, predictable distribution

### Distribution Breakdown

#### Years 0-29 (30-Year Distribution)
- **Total Distribution**: 99.99% of total supply over 30 years
- **Annual Emissions**: Front-loaded first 4 years, then evenly distributed
- **Role Allocation** (equal 20% each):
  - Artists: 20%
  - Listeners: 20%
  - Elderfiers: 20%
  - ABLE/DAI Liquidity Providers: 20%
  - PARA/DAI Liquidity Providers: 20%

#### Emission Timeline (Actual PARA Amounts):
- **Year 0**: 0 QT (Pre-mined, no emission)
- **Year 1**: 100 QT total (20 QT each to Artists, Listeners, Elderfiers, ABLE/DAI LPs, PARA/DAI LPs)
- **Year 2**: 80 QT total (16 QT each to all roles)
- **Year 3**: 60 QT total (12 QT each to all roles)
- **Year 4**: 40 QT total (8 QT each to all roles)
- **Years 5-29**: ~34 QT per year (6.8 QT each to all roles)
- **Year 30**: 14 QT total (2.8 QT each to all roles)
- **Final Result**: 999.9 QT distributed (99.99% of total supply)
- **Design Philosophy**: Front-loaded growth incentives, then sustainable generational distribution
